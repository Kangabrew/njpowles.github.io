Skippy's Auto Service was started by an Australian immigrant who was struggling financially and needed a side job but wanted to help people by completing services on vehicles for an affordable price. Understanding that most people don't have a lot to spend to get a mechanic shop for services, Skippy's Auto Service will come to you. At work with a busy life? no problem SAS will come handle your service while you're at work! Being able to help people financially and mechanically is the goal.
